A little about me and this blog

Hello! Welcome to this space. I enjoy travel, draft beer, staying fit and building cool things. I work as a Technical Account Manager for advertising platforms at Google and I have an MBA from NYU Stern School of Business. Before joining Google, I used to work at Goldman Sachs as a software developer and Charles River Development as a Technical Implementation Manager for electronic trading platforms.

I am also a certified CrossFit Level 1 Trainer and a fitness instructor at Google.

I like to write about a diverse set of topics. Observations, technology trends, data-driven experiments, hacks and travels. Some of my posts are fairly technical, but I try and write for an audience who has taken at least one computer science class in their life. Feedback always appreciated. A quick note on gender. I often use the term 'he' as a convenience. Please feel free to ':%s/he/she/g'. My apologies if this bothers you.
