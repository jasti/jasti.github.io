San Diego Visitor Notes

For you visitors coming from out of town, here are some cool picks for things to do in North County (near our wedding).

Restaurants

Mozy’s Cafe - Leucadia - amazing Carribean burritos
Juanita’s Taco Shop - Leucadia - local favorite hole in the wall Mexican joint
Lotus Cafe - Encinitas - delicious low key vegetarian joint
Chart House - Cardiff, La Jolla - Rick worked there for 10 years. Beautiful views and yummy seafood & steak.
Vintana - Escondido - great high end restaurant near Stone Brewery
Pollos Maria - Just like Los Pollos Hermanos, but this one exists for real

Cafes / Dessert

Pannikin - Leucadia - great coffee shop and local hangout
Lofty Coffee Co - Encinitas - might be the best coffee in the area
Chuao Chocolatier Cafe - Encinitas - yummy chocolate store
Elizabethan Desserts - Encinitas - best brownies EVER = their mexican brownies

Bars

Union Kitchen & Tap - Encinitas - laid back bar near Swami’s
Pizza Port - Tasty Grub & Grog - long-time favorite pub for pizza & beers, the Solona Beach location is the original, but they have one in Carlsbad as well
El Callejon - Encinitas - right next to the beach (and where we’re having the wedding reception), incredible margaritas

Activities

CONCEPT Surf Shop - Encinitas - rentals for surf boards and bikes next to Moonlight Beach
Local Surf Spots - Ponto (Carlsbad, right next to the Hilton), D Street (Encinitas, right next to the Days Inn), Beacons (Leucadia, between Carlsbad and Encinitas), and Swamis are the great local surf spots
Carlsbad Outlet Mall - Carlsbad - a really nice outlet mall
K1 Racing - Carlsbad - fun slot car racing (cars go ~30 mph, it’s pretty fun & intense!)
